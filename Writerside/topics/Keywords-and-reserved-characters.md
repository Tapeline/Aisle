# Keywords and reserved characters

Following keywords are defined by Aisle:
- `scope`
- `project`
- `context`
- `containers`
- `deployment`
- `actor`
- `service`
- `system`
- `tech`
- `links`
- `over`
- `external`
- `legend`
- `deploy`

Following characters are grammatically meaningful, hence reserved:
- `(`
- `)`
- `[`
- `]`
- `:`
- `=`
- `-`
- `<`
- `>`
